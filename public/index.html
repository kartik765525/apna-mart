Privacy Policy

Setu Mart

Last updated: April 2026

We respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it to provide grocery and daily needs delivery services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name and mobile number
  • Delivery address, village, and landmark
  • Order details and cart items
  • Payment method such as Cash on Delivery or UPI
  • Location/GPS details at the time of placing an order

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your orders
  • To contact you about order confirmation, delivery, or support
  • To show your order history and delivery status
  • To improve our app, website, products, and services

3. Location Usage

We use your location only to identify the delivery address and help the delivery partner reach you. We do not sell your location data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited order, phone, address, and location details with our delivery partner only for completing your order.

5. Payments

For UPI payments, the payment is completed through the UPI app selected by the customer. We do not store sensitive banking details such as card numbers, UPI PIN, or bank passwords.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable safety steps to protect customer information. However, no online system is 100% secure, so users should also keep their phone and account access safe.

7. User Rights

You can request profile updates, account deletion, or data removal by contacting our support team.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our service is intended for general grocery ordering. Children should use the app only with permission from a parent or guardian.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy when required. The latest version will be available inside the Profile section of the app/website.

10. Contact Us

For privacy or support questions, contact us on WhatsApp or call: 7518576269.
